ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Marie and I live and love digital. I built my first webpage when I was 12. I built numerous fanpages. The time for college came and I completed degrees in Economics and Accountancy and even passed the CPA licensure exams. I thought it was the practical thing to do! All throughout university, I built webpages for my student organizations, student council... it was early days and I hadn't realized my website building hobby could actually be a career!

I then co-founded a web consulting firm in Makati. Worked a lot of Wordpress and custom nonprofit and small business websites (front-end, back-end), did project management, handled accounts, ran the office. I then transferred client-side. I implemented digital projects for Avon ladies and kickstarted their digital strategy in the Philippines.

I'm currently on the lookout for digital projects in Toronto, so if you know of any, give me a buzz.
